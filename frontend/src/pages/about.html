About Amkyaw AI

Your intelligent assistant powered by advanced AI technology

Our Mission

Amkyaw AI Agent is designed to be your personal AI assistant, helping you with a wide range of tasks through natural conversation. We believe in making AI accessible and useful for everyone.

Technology

Built with modern web technologies and powered by state-of-the-art AI models:

  • Frontend: Vanilla JavaScript with a clean, minimalist design
  • Backend: FastAPI (Python) for robust API handling
  • Database: Neon PostgreSQL for reliable data storage
  • AI: Advanced language models for natural interactions

Features

Amkyaw offers a comprehensive set of capabilities:

  • Natural language conversations
  • Code assistance and debugging
  • Research and information retrieval
  • Writing and editing support
  • File processing and analysis
  • Multi-language support

Design Philosophy

We believe in simplicity and elegance. The minimalist design ensures that you can focus on what matters most — your conversations with AI.

"Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication." — Leonardo da Vinci
